Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will you bring your own cleaning supplies?

    Yes, we always come prepared to every cleaning with everything we need.

  • Will you use my products if I would prefer?

    Of course! If you have specific products that you prefer and we do not carry we will gladly use them.

  • How long does a cleaning take?

    That depends on the size of your home or property. The first cleaning will always take longer and the follow up regularly scheduled cleanings will take less time.

  • Do I have to sign a contract?

    No, we will come and clean your home for as long or short as you'd like. Our goal is to work with you for many years!

  • Do I have to be home when you are cleaning?

    No, as long as you have a way to let us in when you are not home you do not have to be there. However, if it makes you feel more comfortable you are welcome to stay and watch what we do. All of our house cleaning employees are trustworthy and friendly.
